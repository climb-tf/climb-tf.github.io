# Introduction
Words you may come across in kz related discussion.

- Table of Contents
{:toc}

## Perf
When you jump on the same tick that you land it is called a perf. Perfs preserve speed and can be used to augment jump height.  

## Bhop/Bunnyhop
A bhop is a jumpstat that involves jumping, hitting a perf, and landing, all on platforms of the same height.  
Bhop is also used as a synonym for perf.  
The way bkz maps are traversed is sometimes called bhopping even though it doesn't require perfs.  

## Mbh
Mbh is a stat that involves chaining perfs on platforms of the same height.  
Mbh also means any use of chaining perfs.  

## Stat
Stat is short for jumpstat.  
Statters, as opposed to map runners, are players who primarily play for jumpstats.  

## Pre
Pre is short for "pre-speed", the speed you get by running before a long jump or air strafing before a perf.

## Deadstrafe
A deadstrafe is a slow air strafe done during the period of reduced air-acceleration.  

## Ctap/Crouch tap/MJ/Mini jump/Low jump
A ctap is a jump with reduced height executed by jumping while uncrouching.  

## Glidestep/Ledgegrab
When airborne, uncrouching onto a platform instantly grounds you. This is called glidestepping.  

## Surf
Surfing is maintaining height by strafing into a sloped surface.  
A surf up, also known as a slide, is a very steep slope that can be climbed.  

## Hsw/Hsw pre
Hsw is short for half-sideways.  
Air strafing half-sideways involves holding W and a strafe key instead of just the strafe key.  
Building pre half-sideways involves running diagonally with W and a strafe key. This is useful for getting high pre and a good angle on thin ledges.  

## Edge
Edge in KZ refers to the space between the edge of a platform and where jump is pressed. "Good" edge is a small amount of space.  

## Sync
In a jumpstat, sync is the percentage of the time mouse movements match strafe key presses.  

## Wall strafe
A wall strafe is a distant jump done with perfect airpath along a wall. It is executed by looking roughly 4 degrees away from the wall and strafing into it.
